The AVeriTeC challenge aims to evaluate the ability of systems to verify real-world claims with evidence from the Web.

The AVeriTeC shared task is part of the 8th FEVER Workshop, co-located with ACL 2025 and will be held in Vienna, Austria in July-August 2025.

The systems in the shared task have to perform the following:

  • Given a claim and its metadata, the systems must retrieve evidence that supports and/or refutes the claim, either from the Web or from the document collection provided.
  • Using this evidence, label the claim as Supported, Refuted given the evidence, Not Enough Info (if there isn't sufficient evidence to either support or refute it) or Conflicting Evidence/Cherrypicking (if the claim has both supporting and refuting evidence).

      • To learn more about the task and our baseline implementation, read our paper AVERITEC: A Dataset for Real-world Claim Verification with Evidence from the Web and go to the shared task webpage.
